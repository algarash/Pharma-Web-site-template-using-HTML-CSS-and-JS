About Us

History

The history of Pharma Soul Group goes back to the early 2012s. It started as a wholesale company, and due to its long experience in medicines and medical products, it was decided to establish an importing and distributing company to fulfill the needs of the national market of medicines and medical products.

The extensive sales and marketing background we acquired over the past 15 years ensures our full recognition of the range of needs of pharmaceutical markets in Yemen.
We had always been to establish a business entity whereby we could be able to utilize this expertise and marketing skills with cooperating with selective principals who are the partners in success.

Thus,Pharma Soul Group was founded in the year 2002 with the fundamental policy of developing a mutually profitable and healthy long term relationship with its principals and customers by providing high quality products and services.

Sections

Pharma Soul Group Consists of Several Sections Including:

  1. Medications and Pharmaceutical Finished Formulations
  2. Food Supplements
  3. Cosmetics
  4. Healty Care Product
  5. Nutrition Products

Mission

Pharma Soul is a powerful multi disciplinary business organization with full access to the techniques of modern business and technological arena.

It is engaged in wholesale business, importation and distribution of pharmaceuticals.
Our product ranges for the pharmaceuticals, health care, different baby and pediatric product and various medical products which improve the quality of our nation's life.

Pharma Soul Group, is young company in all terms, in its associates (employees), in coping with high technology, and at the same time has the experience in sales and marketing of pharmaceuticals and medical products.

Pharma Soul became fully operated in the year 2002 to meet the challenges of growing and highly competitive market. Pharma Soul is ready to pursue an active and effective role in the global pharmaceutical market through professional management, proper utilization of marketing resources and emphasis on the customer service concept.

Pharma Soul integrates the latest enterprise resource planning (ERP) and stock management software to ensure that customer orders are managed, processed and delivered efficiently and accurately.

Pharma Soul also uses its ERP and comprehensive employee screening and management system to mange it’s 63+ employees.Pharma Soul also invests in continuous training.

programs to ensure that its service and marketing team is up to date with the latest information and standards.

Pharma Soul’s customers include all the largest government and private hospitals; however, it’s not only limited to large organizations.
Pharma Soul has the capabilities to do business on all scales from single consumer orders to turn key projects.

Pharma Soul has always strived to implement a clear and transparent line between its customers and employees ensuring that comments and constructive criticism is always heard. This in turn, has added in developing Pharma Soul philosophy of performance and progress.

Vision

Our vision at Pharma Soul Group is to offer though our principals the most innovative and highest quality products and services to improve the quality of life of the patients and to take care of every single aspect of health care and to concentrate on the niche marketing on the General, Gynecological, pediatric and Dermatological markets.
